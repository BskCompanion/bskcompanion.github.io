Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

BSK Companion respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

Information We Collect

BSK Companion does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may access non-personal information required for basic functionality, including internet connectivity and device-related data.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as advertising or analytics providers. These services may collect non-personal information including device type, IP address, and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain app functionality
  • To enable online features and downloads
  • To improve performance and stability

BSK Companion does not sell, rent, or knowingly share personal data with third parties.

Downloads and Local Storage

Any files downloaded through the app are stored locally on the user’s device. The app does not access personal files outside its intended functionality.

Children’s Privacy

BSK Companion is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the app and its functionality. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
